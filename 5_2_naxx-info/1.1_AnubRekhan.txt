{Skull}Anub'Rekhan{Skull}

Adds:
- Crypt Guards - Elite
    ACID SPIT (dot stacks indefinitely from every attack) - offtanks may need GNPP
    CLEAVE, ENRAGE at 50% and aoe WEB
- Corpse scarabs - Non-elite

Abilities:
- IMPALE: Line of spikes at random player within 35 yards, 4000dmg on cloth. Everyone hit is thrown in the air and takes about 1000dmg.
- SPAWN CORPSE SCARABS: Everytime a player or Crypt Guard dies, it will spawn 10 corpse scarabs.
- LOCUST SWARM: 
    30 yard AOE from Anub that affects everyone in range.
    Silenceing and doing massive dmg. 
    Anub has 40% movement during locust swarm.
    Used every 70-120sec.

Tactics:
- 4 tanks: 1 on Anub, 1 on CG1, 2 on CG2 - kill crypt guards first.
- Remember to kill all scarabs that spawn from bodies
- Spread out to avoid IMPALE the whole raid.
- LOCUST SWARM:
    Move AWAY from Anub - group in center of room and kill Crypt Guards that spawn (2 tanks - 3 tanks if 2x at once)
    MT kites Anub in a half circle around ledge back to the entrance - no dps during this unless out of range of swarm
